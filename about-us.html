HISTORY OF SWAGAT PRODUCT

With over 15 years of rich experience and deep understanding of the dairy industry, Swagat Paroduct was incorporated in 2009 with the sole aim of producing and providing the citizens of India with the finest and purest ghee.
Since then, we have just grown multifold with the consistent efforts of our talented team and the wholehearted love from our customers that has helped Swagat product become a name to reckon with in a short span of time.

INFRASTRUCTURE

Our head office is situated in the Chhatral (Gandhinagar, Gujarat) . Our production unit located at Chhatral is more than 50,000 sq. Feet in size and has the ability to handle production capacity of approximately 10 tonnes per day.
