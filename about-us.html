OVERVIEW ABOUT US

SADHVIK PRODUCTS

At siddhraj food product Sadhvik Pure Ghee, we are committed to bringing you the purest and most authentic ghee, made from high-quality milk sourced directly from cow and buffalo stables.
We follow a meticulous process to ensure the traditional richness and nutritional value of our ghee is preserved.

Our Process

Quality Milk Procurement

We partner with local stables to collect fresh, wholesome milk from healthy cows and buffaloes. Our milk is sourced ethically, ensuring the highest standards of quality

Traditional Processing

The milk is carefully processed using time-honored techniques that retain its natural flavor and nutritional properties. We churn the milk into butter, which is then clarified into pure ghee using a slow, precise method.

Purity Guaranteed

Our ghee is free from additives, preservatives, and artificial flavors. Each batch is tested for quality, giving you a product that is rich in aroma, flavor, and health benefits.

Our Commitment to Hygiene and Quality

At Sadhvik Pure Ghee, we understand the importance of maintaining the highest standards of hygiene throughout the ghee-making process. From the moment the milk is sourced to the final stage of packaging, every step is carried out in a controlled, sanitary environment.

Sterile Collection and Storage

We ensure that the milk we collect from cow and buffalo stables is transported and stored in sterilized containers, maintaining its freshness and purity.

Hygienic Processing

Our production facility follows strict cleanliness protocols. All equipment is regularly sanitized, and our team follows proper hygiene practices, including wearing protective gear during the entire process.

Safe Packaging

Once the ghee is processed, it is immediately packed in hygienic, airtight containers to preserve its quality and flavor, ensuring that what reaches you is fresh, pure, and safe to consume.
